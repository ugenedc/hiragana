Study Japanese Anywhere: Tips for Learning While Traveling

April 8, 2024
Study Tips
By Eugene D'Hage-Craig

Life gets busy, but that shouldn't stop you from your goal to learn Japanese. Whether you're commuting, waiting, or traveling, you can turn downtime into productive study time. Here's how to study Japanese anywhere, anytime.

1. Embrace Mobile Learning Apps

Your smartphone is a powerful tool to learn Japanese on the go. Use apps like Kanabloom for quick Hiragana and Katakana flashcard sessions. Download lessons or materials for offline access during flights or commutes.

2. Utilize Flashcards (Digital or Physical)

Flashcards are perfect for bite-sized learning. Keep a small deck of physical cards or use a Japanese flashcards app. Review characters or vocabulary during short breaks throughout your day.

3. Listen to Japanese Audio

Use commute time or waiting periods to listen to Japanese podcasts, music, or audio lessons. Passive listening helps with pronunciation and comprehension, even if you're not actively studying.

4. Set Mini-Goals for Downtime

Have realistic expectations. Instead of aiming for long study sessions, set small, achievable goals for your travel or waiting time. Examples: Learn 3 new Katakana characters, review 10 flashcards, listen to one podcast episode.

5. Practice Mentally

Even without materials, you can practice Japanese. Try mentally translating signs you see, forming simple sentences in your head, or recalling vocabulary related to your surroundings.

6. Bring a Small Notebook

Carry a small notebook and pen to jot down new words you encounter, practice writing Hiragana or Katakana, or note down questions to look up later. Active recall through writing is very effective.

Conclusion: Make Every Moment Count

Learning Japanese doesn't require hours chained to a desk. By leveraging mobile apps like Kanabloom for Hiragana flashcards, utilizing audio resources, and setting mini-goals, you can effectively study Japanese anywhere. Turn travel time and downtime into valuable learning opportunities!
