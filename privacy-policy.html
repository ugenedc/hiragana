Privacy Policy

Effective as of March 27, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Kanabloom app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by MOST VALUABLE PRODUCT PTY LTD (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects minimal information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your learning progress and statistics
  • Custom flashcard sets you create
  • App usage statistics (time spent practicing, completion rates)
  • Basic device information (OS version, device type)

Data Storage and Processing

All your learning data, including custom flashcards and progress, is stored locally on your device. The Application does not require user accounts or cloud storage. Your personal learning data remains private and under your control.

Analytics and Performance Data

The Application collects anonymous usage data to improve the learning experience:

  • Performance Metrics: How users interact with different learning modules and features
  • Error Reporting: Technical information if the app encounters problems
  • Feature Usage: Which learning methods are most effective for users

Third Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store services for app distribution
  • Basic analytics tools to improve app performance
  • Crash reporting tools to identify and fix technical issues

These services receive only anonymized, aggregated data and cannot identify individual users.

Your Data Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • All learning data is stored locally and can be deleted by uninstalling the app
  • No personal information is required to use the app
  • You can reset your progress at any time within the app

Data Retention

Your learning data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. Uninstalling the Application will remove all locally stored data. The Service Provider retains anonymous analytics data for app improvement purposes for a reasonable period.

Children's Privacy

While the Application is suitable for learners of all ages, it does not collect any personally identifiable information. Users under 13 should have parental consent to use the Application. The app does not include any social features or ways to communicate with other users.

App Permissions

The Application requires minimal device permissions:

  • Storage access (to save your learning progress)
  • Internet access (for downloading updates and sending anonymous analytics)

No additional permissions such as location, contacts, or camera access are required.

Security

The Service Provider implements appropriate security measures to protect your learning data:

  • All data is stored securely on your device
  • Analytics data is transmitted using secure protocols
  • No sensitive personal information is collected or stored

Updates to Privacy Policy

The Service Provider may update this privacy policy to reflect app improvements and new features. Users will be notified of significant changes through app updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about your privacy while using the Application, please contact the Service Provider at ugenedc@gmail.com.
