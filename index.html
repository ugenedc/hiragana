Frequently Asked Questions about Learning Hiragana & Katakana

How long does it take to learn Hiragana?

Most learners can memorize Hiragana in 1–2 weeks with daily practice. Kanabloom’s spaced repetition flashcards help you retain characters faster by focusing on the ones you miss.

Should I learn Hiragana before Katakana?

Yes. Start with Hiragana for native Japanese words, then Katakana for loanwords. Kanabloom includes dedicated modes for each to build a strong foundation.

What’s the best way to practice Japanese characters?

Short, consistent sessions with active recall and spaced repetition are best. Our revision mode automatically schedules reviews to maximize retention.
